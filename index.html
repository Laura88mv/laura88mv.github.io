Recent Work

Campaign Overview


Created, managed and executed marketing campaign for “Women of Yoli”. The goal was to create a community around our brand, increase engagement, and cultivate a strong and loyal customer base.

Objective

The primary goal of the campaign was to foster a sense of community among the female audience associated with the Yoli brand.


Target Audience

The campaign specifically targeted women between 25 to 45 years old, aiming to resonate with their values, interests, and healthy lifestyle.

Strategies and Tactics

  • Content Strategy: Developed a content strategy centered around empowering and inspiring women. Created diverse content types, including blog posts, images, videos, and interactive content to cater to various preferences.

  • Engagement Initiatives: Implemented interactive elements such as polls, quizzes, and challenges to encourage active participation. Facilitated discussions around topics relevant to the target audience, promoting engagement and dialogue.

  • Collaborations: Forged partnerships with influencers and thought leaders within the women's community to extend reach and credibility. Collaborated with like-minded brands for cross-promotion, broadening the campaign's impact.

  • Content (UGC): Encouraged followers to share their Yoli experiences, stories, and photos, amplifying user-generated content. Curated and showcased UGC to highlight the diversity and authenticity of the community.
